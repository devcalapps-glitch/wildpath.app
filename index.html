WildPath

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

WildPath is built to keep your trip planning on your device. We do not run user accounts, and we do not collect, sell, or store your trip data on our servers.

Information We Store Locally

The information you enter in WildPath is saved on your device only, including:

  • Your name and optional email address
  • Trip details, dates, locations, and notes
  • Gear lists and meal plans
  • Budget entries
  • Emergency contacts
  • Camping style preferences
  • Pass and permit information

This data is stored in your device's local app storage. If you uninstall WildPath, that local data is removed from the device and cannot be recovered by us.

Location Information

WildPath may use the campsite or trip location you enter to show your trip on the map and to support weather and alert features.

If you choose to use device location for map features, WildPath will request permission to access your current location. That location is used only within the app to help place you on the map and is not stored by WildPath.

Third-Party Services

WildPath uses a few third-party services to provide core features:

  • Google Places to search and resolve campsite locations
  • Open-Meteo to fetch weather forecasts
  • National Weather Service / weather.gov to check for weather alerts
  • WeatherAPI, when configured, as an optional alerts source

When you search for a place or view weather for a trip, the information needed for that feature may be sent to the relevant service. These providers handle their own data under their own privacy policies.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, WildPath may use your saved trip information to send trip reminders and weather alert notifications on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally.

Data Retention

Your data stays on your device until you remove it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. WildPath does not have a way to restore deleted local data.

Children's Privacy

WildPath is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page.
