Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: April 2025

WildPath is built on a simple promise: your adventure data is yours. We collect only what we need to make the app work, never sell your personal information, and give you full control over what you share.

What we collect

To provide WildPath's services, we collect:

  • Account information (name, email address) when you sign up
  • Location data when you use map or navigation features (with your permission)
  • Trip plans, saved campsites, and notes you create in the app
  • Device identifiers and crash logs to help us fix bugs
  • Usage analytics (anonymized) to understand how features are used

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver and personalize the WildPath experience
  • Sync your trips and plans across devices
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Send account-related notifications (you can opt out anytime)
  • Provide customer support when you reach out to us

We do not use your data to serve advertising or build behavioral profiles for third parties.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with essential service providers (cloud hosting, crash analytics) under strict data processing agreements, and only when legally required by valid legal process.

Location data

WildPath requests location access to show nearby campsites, enable navigation, and provide weather specific to where you are. You can grant "while using" or "always on" access — or deny it entirely. Core features like trip planning work without location access. Location data is never stored on our servers without your consent.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your trip data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Submit requests via Settings → Account → Privacy, or email us directly.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We conduct regular security audits and follow responsible disclosure practices. If you discover a security issue, please contact us at dev.cal.apps@gmail.com.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach our privacy team at dev.cal.apps@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

About us

Built by explorers,
for explorers

WildPath started around a campfire in 2023 when our founders kept running into the same problem: planning a great camping trip took hours across a dozen different apps and websites.

We set out to build one beautifully simple tool that handles everything — from discovering your first campsite to navigating backcountry trails offline. No bloat. Just adventure.

  • We believe the outdoors should be accessible to everyone, not just experts.
  • We're committed to Leave No Trace and environmental stewardship.
  • We build for global adventurers — WildPath works in 120+ countries.
  • We'll never put ad revenue above your privacy or your experience.
Our mission
"To make every wild place feel less intimidating and more inviting — so more people can experience the healing power of spending time in nature."
